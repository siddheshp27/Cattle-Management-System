All Cattle

This feature helps the farmer understand the details and strength of the cattle farm they own. It displays list off all cattle they own and their details like eartag number, stage, breed, etc. It helps the farmer understand how many cattle they have in a specific stage. There is a button below the navbar named "New Registration" which will redirect the farmer to the new cattle registration form.

New Cattle Registration

This form helps the farmer register new cattle into the database. The farmer needs to fill out the form according to the specifications. If the farmer does not know the breed of the cattle they are expected to put in "unknown" and not leave the space empty. After they are done filling out the form they must click on the "submit" button to save the form entries.

Morning Milk Production

This feature helps the farmer record the input of milk produced in the morning for a day by a specific cow. The farmer is expected to enter value for one cow, submit it and then move on to the next cow. The entry should be in Liters.
